Silence

November 2025

Silence is not empty. It's full of answers.

We fill every gap with noise—music, podcasts, notifications. As if silence were something to be avoided. But silence is where thoughts complete themselves.

In conversation, the pause is where understanding lives. In music, the rest gives the note its meaning. In design, the whitespace lets the eye breathe.

What you don't say matters as much as what you do.
